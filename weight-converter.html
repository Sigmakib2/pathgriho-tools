All About Weight Conversion:

A body's relative mass or the quantity of matter contained by the body, giving rise to a downward force is called weight. We need to use weight in every spare of life. There is no need to talk about the usage of weight. But some may can ask, “what is the importance of conversion of weight in measurement?” But the answer is that reason you came here to convert for. Weight conversion is also important for keeping records. Mainly in different areas of world different units are being followed to measure something’s weight. Some follow the metric system where the others follow non-metric. Whatever here we are going to explain almost every way to convert weight.

1.Converting Grams to Kilograms, Milligrams, Pounds and Ounces

1A. How to Convert Grams to Kilograms?

1000 gram is equal to 1 Kilogram. Again 1 gram is equals to 0.001 Kilograms (kg).
Thus,
1 g=(1/1000) kg=0.001 kg
In one word, it can be calculating in the formation given below:
m (gram) / 1000=M (kg)

Example 1:
Covert 10 g to Kilogram.
10 (g) / 1000=0.01 kg

1B. How to Convert Grams to Milligrams?

1000 mg is equals to 1 gram.
So,
1 g=1000 mg
In short it can be express in the formation given below:
M (g) × 1000=m (mg)

Example 2:
Covert 10 g to milligrams.
10 (g) × 1000=10000 kg

1C. How to covert Grams to Pounds?

We know that, 1 gram is equal to 0.00220462262185 pounds. Pounds is denoted by lbs.
Therefore,
1 g=0.00220462262185 lb
In short,
m(lb)=M(g) / 453.59237

Example 3:
Convert 10g to pounds:
m(lb)=10 g / 453.59237=0.02204622 lb

1D. How to covert Grams to Ounces?

It is known that, 1 gram (g) is equal to 0.03527396195 ounces. Ounces is denoted by oz.
Thus,
1 g=0.03527396195 oz
In short
m(oz)=M(g) / 28.34952

Example4:
Convert 15g to Ounces:
m(oz)=15 g / 28.34952=0.529109 oz
