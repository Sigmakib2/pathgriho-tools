Body Mass Index Calculator

BMI or Body Mass Index refers to the ratio of a person’s weight in kg and the square of height in meters. It indicates a person’s health condition. It may be used to screen for weight categories which can lead a person to health problems. It actually tries to declare if a man or women is thin, healthy or having over weight.

Formula: Body Mass Index (BMI) = Weight in Kilograms/ (Height in meters)2
