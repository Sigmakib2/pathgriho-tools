The Relation between Celsius, Fahrenheit and Kelvin are: Celsius/5 = (Fahrenheit – 32)/9 = (Kelvin – 273)/5

Starting point of Celsius is 0 degree Celsius and the ending point is 100 degree. Whereas these two values for Fahrenheit and Kelvin are 32, 180 and 273.15, 373.15.
