Free Online Age Calculator

It is not important to say “what is age?”, but it is important to answer another question related to age. And the question is, how to calculate age? The age of a person or an institution can be counted in so many different ways. It differs according to cultures. But most countries follow a system and that is marked as the standard way. ToolKib’s this tool is made based on that system.

ToolKib’s Free Online Age Calculator needs only the birthdate (date, month and year) to count. It has the present day’s date automatically. If you want to calculate to other dates, you can also change the dates according to your requirements. And then, you just need to click on the Calculate Age button. And the tool will calculate your age instantly.

For example, if a man born in 15th May 1982, in 3rd December 3, 2020 his age will be 38 years 6 months and 18 days.
