Feet and Inch to Meters and Centimeters

Sometimes, we need to convert our height to meters. But most of the time we measure our height in feet and inch. But for so many purposes we need to convert. For example, if we want to calculate Body Mass Index (Calculate BMI with ToolKib)( you need your height in meters.

We know, 1 meter = feet/3.2808

1 meter = inch/39.379

1 meter = 100 centimeter

For example, 5 feet and 5 inch is equivalent to 1.651 meters and 165.1 centimeter.
